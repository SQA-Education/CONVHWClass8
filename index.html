1. Conversation Awareness

Presenting a Business Idea to Investors

Investors usually want entrepreneurs to explain their business idea clearly and show how it can grow.

A simple business plan often includes the problem, the solution, the target customers, and the revenue model.

An elevator pitch is a short explanation of the business idea that should be clear, persuasive, and easy to remember.
